BIRTH CERTIFICATE
Birth certificates are needed for a variety of reasons, including school/sports, passports, and driver license . As a Colorado government agency, we are authorized to issue birth certificates to you directly. You do not need to go through a third-party agency.

WHO CAN ORDER

Copies of Colorado Birth Certificates will only be issued to persons named on the record, other members of the immediate family, a legal representative of the person named on the record, or others demonstrating a direct and tangible interest in the record. Proof of relationship or direct and tangible interest is required of anyone not named on the record.

Photo ID (non expired) is require for all request. See eligibility and identification criteria on upload your identification screen. Instructions will be provided at the time when you will upload your identification. Colorado law requires you to have a “tangible interest” in a person’s birth or death certificate before we release this document to you. This helps protect against fraud by ensuring this personal information is only released to authorized persons for legitimate reasons.

By signing below, you acknowledge that you have a tangible interest in the documents you are requesting, and are aware of the penalties for obtaining a birth or death certificate under false pretenses. If you have additional questions, please feel free to call our office at 303-602-3660.

BY CONTINUING, I UNDERSTAND Pursuant to Colorado Revised Statutes, 1982, 25-2-2118 and as defined by Colorado Board of Health Rules and regulations, applicant must have a direct and tangible interest in the record requested. The penalties for obtaining a record under false pretenses include a fine of not more than $1,000 or imprisonment in the county jail for not more than one year or both such fine and imprisonment (CRS 25-2-2118).
